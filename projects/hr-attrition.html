HR Attrition & Workforce Analytics

Interactive Power BI dashboard predicting employee attrition patterns and enabling data-driven retention strategies.

Problem Statement

Organizations struggle to predict employee attrition and understand risk factors. This dashboard provides interactive insights for proactive retention strategies and workforce planning.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI DAX Data Modeling KPI Design

Your Role & Responsibilities

  • Analyzed attrition patterns across departments, salary bands, job roles, and employee tenure
  • Designed interactive dashboards with slicers, drill-downs, and KPI visualizations
  • Created advanced DAX measures for attrition rates, retention metrics, and workforce analytics
  • Delivered executive-ready insights for HR leadership decision-making

Dashboard Flow

KPIs → Department & Salary Analysis → Tenure Trends → Executive Summary

Key Insights

  • Highest attrition risk in Technology & Sales departments
  • Medium salary band employees show highest exit rates
  • Early tenure (0–3 years) identified as critical risk period
  • Interactive slicers enable real-time scenario analysis

Role Mapping

  • Data Analyst: KPI design, DAX measures, actionable insights
  • Python Developer: Structured analytical logic and data flows
  • AI/ML: Feature analysis for future predictive modeling
